Living, learning, working, creating.

Victor is an independent graphic designer creating things with or without computers, mostly alongside a good cup of tea and some rap music. Everything begins with an idea... regardless of whether it ends up as a piece of print or a screen full of code. With skills in both tactile and digital environments I enjoy experimenting with different tools to create something from nothing.

Currently based in Leeds and working on various freelance and personal projects. I am always seeking new opportunities and collaborations, so be sure to say hello...

What?

I have created logos and branding, design for print including posters, editorial work, packaging, flyers and direct mail. Also front end web development and design for digital applications including icons, responsive layouts and social media elements.

I have worked with local agencies such as Impression DP, WTS Technologies and charities such as Mayfield Farm, Leeds City of Sanctuary. Creatives such as Mark Tattersall Photography, Lydia Leith, Maps of Colombus.
